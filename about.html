Our Story

A Passion for Great Seafood

Bernard and Rene Edwards started B&R Seafood and More with a simple mission: bring the best golden fried seafood and authentic Southern sides to the community. What began as a shared love of cooking for family and friends has grown into something the whole community can enjoy.

Every recipe at B&R Seafood is crafted with love and seasoned with tradition. We take pride in using quality ingredients and time-tested techniques to deliver flavors that remind you of home cooking at its finest.

Family Tradition

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, blending Southern cooking traditions with our own family's unique flavors. Every dish we serve carries a piece of our heritage.

Cooked Fresh Daily

We believe great food starts with quality ingredients and careful preparation. Our shrimp and catfish are golden fried to perfection, and our sides are made from scratch every single day.

Serving Our Community

From local events to your front door, we're proud to serve our neighbors. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite or feeding the whole family, B&R Seafood and More has you covered.
