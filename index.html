Our Story

A Passion for Great Seafood

Family Tradition

Bernard and Rene Edwards started B&R Seafood and More with a simple mission: bring the best golden fried seafood and authentic Southern sides to the community. Every recipe is crafted with love and seasoned with tradition.

Cooked Fresh Daily

We believe great food starts with quality ingredients and careful preparation. Our shrimp and catfish are golden fried to perfection, and our sides are made from scratch with recipes passed down through generations.

Serving Our Community

From local events to your front door, we're proud to serve our neighbors. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite or feeding the whole family, B&R Seafood and More has you covered.
